Jasprit's Cricket Career

  • Jasprit Bumrah made his international debut for the Indian cricket team in January 2016 in a T20 match against Australia. Since then, he has become one of the premier fast bowlers in world cricket.
  • Bumrah's unorthodox action and ability to bowl yorkers consistently make him a valuable asset in limited-overs cricket.
  • He quickly rose through the ranks to become the spearhead of India's bowling attack in all formats of the game.
  • His ability to perform under pressure in crucial moments of the game has earned him praise from teammates and opponents alike.
  • Bumrah's remarkable success in all formats of the game has cemented his place as one of the best bowlers in modern cricket.

IPL Career

  • Jasprit Bumrah has been an integral part of the Mumbai Indians franchise in the Indian Premier League (IPL) since making his debut in 2013.
  • Known for his ability to bowl accurate yorkers and slower deliveries at the death, Bumrah has been one of the most successful bowlers in the history of the tournament.
  • He has played a crucial role in Mumbai Indians' title-winning campaigns, contributing with both wickets and economical bowling in pressure situations.
  • Bumrah's consistent performances in the IPL have earned him praise from fans and experts alike, and he is regarded as one of the best death bowlers in the world.
