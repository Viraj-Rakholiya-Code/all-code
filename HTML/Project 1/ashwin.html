Ravichandran Ashwin


  • Name: Ravichandran Ashwin

  • Date Of Birth: 17 September 1986

  • Debut: 2010

  • Country: India


Ravichandran Ashwin's Cricket Career

  • Ravichandran Ashwin is one of the premier off-spinners in the history of Indian cricket.
  • He has represented India in all three formats of the game and has been a prolific wicket-taker for the team.
  • Ashwin is known for his ability to spin the ball sharply and deceive even the best batsmen in the world.
  • He has played a crucial role in many memorable victories for the Indian cricket team.

IPL Career

  • Ravichandran Ashwin has been an integral part of various IPL franchises throughout his career.
  • He has played for teams like Chennai Super Kings, Rising Pune Supergiant, Kings XI Punjab, and Delhi Capitals.
  • Ashwin is known for his clever variations and ability to outfox batsmen even on the most batsman-friendly pitches.
  • He has contributed significantly to his team's success in the IPL and has been one of the top performers in the league.

Awards

  • ICC Test Cricketer of the Year (2016)
