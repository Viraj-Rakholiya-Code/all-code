Sachin Tendulkar


  • Name: Sachin Tendulkar

  • Date Of Birth: 24 April 1973

  • Debut: 1989

  • Country: India


Sachin Tendulkar's Cricket Career

  • Sachin Tendulkar is regarded as one of the greatest cricketers of all time.
  • He holds numerous records in international cricket, including the most runs scored in both Test and ODI formats.
  • Tendulkar represented the Indian cricket team for over two decades and was known for his impeccable batting technique and consistency.
  • He played a pivotal role in India's victories in various tournaments, including the ICC Cricket World Cup.
  • Tendulkar's impact on the game of cricket extends beyond statistics, as he inspired millions of aspiring cricketers around the world.

Sachin Tendulkar

IPL Career

  • Sachin Tendulkar played for the Mumbai Indians in the Indian Premier League (IPL).
  • He was the icon player and captain of the Mumbai Indians team.
  • Tendulkar's presence in the IPL added immense value to the Mumbai Indians franchise, both on and off the field.
  • Despite being primarily known for his exploits in international cricket, Tendulkar showcased his batting prowess in the IPL and played some memorable innings.
  • His leadership and experience were instrumental in guiding the Mumbai Indians to their maiden IPL title in 2013.

Awards
