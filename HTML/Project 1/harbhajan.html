Harbhajan Singh


  • Name: Harbhajan Singh

  • Date Of Birth: 3 July 1980

  • Nickname: Bhajji

  • Debut: 1998

  • Country: India


Harbhajan Singh's Cricket Career

  • Harbhajan Singh is one of the most successful off-spinners in the history of Indian cricket.
  • He has represented India in all three formats of the game and has taken numerous wickets for the team.
  • Bhajji, as he is fondly called, has played a crucial role in many memorable victories for the Indian cricket team.
  • He has been known for his ability to spin the ball sharply and outfox even the best batsmen in the world.

Harbhajan Singh

IPL Career

  • Harbhajan Singh has had a successful career in the Indian Premier League (IPL).
  • He has represented several franchises in the tournament, including Mumbai Indians, Chennai Super Kings, and Kolkata Knight Riders.
  • Bhajji has been known for his economical bowling and ability to take wickets in crucial moments during IPL matches.
  • He has played an instrumental role in helping his teams win IPL titles and has been a valuable asset in the league.

Awards

  • Arjuna Award (2003)
  • Padma Shri (2009)
  • ICC Test Player of the Year (2002)
  • ICC World Test XI (2009, 2010, 2011)
  • Indian Cricket Cricketer of the Year (2008)
  • BCCI's Polly Umrigar Award for Best Indian Cricketer (2012)
