Rahul Dravid


  • Name: Rahul Dravid

  • Date Of Birth: 11 January 1973

  • Nickname: The Wall

  • Debut: 1996

  • Country: India


Rahul Dravid's Cricket Career

  • Rahul Dravid is one of the greatest batsmen to have ever played for India.
  • He was known for his impeccable technique, solid defense, and remarkable concentration at the crease.
  • Dravid represented India in both Test and ODI formats and was a key player in the team for over a decade.
  • He scored over 10,000 runs in both Test and ODI cricket and played crucial roles in many memorable victories for India.
  • Dravid's contribution to Indian cricket goes beyond his batting prowess, as he also served as the captain of the team and later as a coach and mentor.

Rahul Dravid

IPL Career

  • Rahul Dravid played in the Indian Premier League (IPL) for the Rajasthan Royals.
  • He was known for his leadership and tactical acumen, which earned him the nickname "The Wall" in the shortest format of the game.
  • Dravid's calm and composed demeanor made him a respected figure both on and off the field during his time in the IPL.
  • Although he was not known for his aggressive style of play, Dravid's contributions were invaluable to the success of the Rajasthan Royals in the IPL.
